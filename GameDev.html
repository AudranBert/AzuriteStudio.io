Portobugia

Project management, Marketing, Communication​, Object-oriented programming, Game design, Entreprenership
See on Steam

Portobugia was made by 2 people using Unreal Engine. It is a game which takes place in eastern part of the Russian empire just after the end of WWI. You are respondible of managing a port during a pandemic. You must unload ships but to do so, you must inspect them to find if they are infected or not. You must supply the city with food and goods. To spice things up, random events will force you to take harsh decision. To improve your survavibility, you can upgrade/construct buildings. You can also research technologies, find old ancient artifacts...

Knights & Cards

Project management, ​Object-oriented programming​, Game design
See on Itch.io

It is tactical game inspired by Fire Emblem and Advance Wars. As other tactical, you manage units and must defeat the ennemy army. But here there are few specificities that makes the game unique. First, compare to the 2 games I cited, there is a management of height and orientation. If you are higher than your ennermy you will have a small bonus. If you face your ennemy you will have a defensive bonus, if he attacks you from the rear you will suffer a defensive penalty. The second unique features is that in order to give orders to units you will have to play cards. Cards can give you offensive/defensive bonuses, the possibility to attack, move or orient. You can create your own deck (a set of cards) through a deck builder. There is a campaign with 10 missions to complete, at the beginning of each mission you select wich units during a deployment phase. Game was never finished (assets still in prototype stages, missing cards/units, and a big part of the campaign is missing).

ClickFever

Object-oriented programming​, Game design
See on Itch.io

ClickFever is small game made in 24 hours. The goal was to made a game fully playable with menus, gameplay and settings in a very short time. I used Unreal engine to do it. 
You must click on colored cubes (each colors gives a different amount of score and time) but you need to avoid to click on sphere (they will remove a life). The goal is to have the higher score and so survive the longest time possible.

Map generator

Python, algorithms, ​object-oriented programming
See on GitHub

This a python project which create maps with tiles with four edges. The generator can be personnalized with there are some parameters. There are different biomes that can spawn on the map such as desert, swamp, ocean... These biomes have rules, for example snow spawn near the poles, mountains tends to spawn next to eachothers in order to make mountain ranges. In the latest version I added resources that can spawn in specific biomes, for example fishes can spawn only in water tiles.  The map which is generated can be exported to html file or text file.
